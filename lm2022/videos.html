Pre-recorded Lecture Videos

The main reference material for this course comprise the following 154 videos. These may be watched online from Youtube or may be downloaded for offline viewing from my Google drive. All the videos are supposed to be watched by all students. However, the student named in square brackets beside each video is responsible for creating a LaTeX document presenting the content of the video. The last date for submission of the LaTeX and pdf files is given inside round brackets. 2 points will be deducted for delays up to 2 days, 5 points for longer delays. All submissions must be done via Google classroom. You MUST submit both the LaTeX as well as the pdf files. Your LaTeX file must not contain any path specific to your machine.

All the diagrams and images used in the videos are available for download from this drive link (updated!). All matrices, equations and formulas MUST be typeset in LaTeX (and not pasted as images).

The pdf's linked below against the videos are those submitted by the students. Be warned that some of them may contain only a subset of the information present in the videos.
